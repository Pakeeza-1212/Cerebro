Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is mental health?

Mental health includes our emotional, psychological, and social well-being. It affects how we think, feel, and act in our daily lives.

How can I seek help for mental health issues?

You can start by reaching out to a mental health professional, talking to a trusted friend or family member, or exploring resources on our website.

Are your services free?

Many of the resources we offer are free, but some specialized services may involve fees. Check individual resources for details.

How do I support a loved one struggling with mental health?

Be patient, listen without judgment, and encourage them to seek professional help. Educate yourself about mental health to better understand their experience.
